My Hobby: Horse‐Riding

I ride every Tuesday near to where I live, at the Clearwater Bay Equestrian Center. Everyone says I am the happiest when I ride. Recently I have taken up a livery on my favorite horse, Bruno. Bruno has brown and white patches on him, sort of like a cow actually! We spend every Tuesday morning together, grooming, bathing, chatting, riding and just having a good time together.

Recently, I have participated in a dressage competition together with Bruno. Though we didn't win together, it was certainly good practice and it was all team work. I look forward to doing more with Bruno, like jumping.

With Covid‐19 restrictions in place, the equestrian center had to close. It has been nearly two months since I last saw Bruno. I am sure Bruno misses me (me = carrots). The center will reopen soon, and I very much look forward to reunite with Bruno and just hope he still recognizes me.
